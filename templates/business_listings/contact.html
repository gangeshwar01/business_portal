Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I add my business?

Simply create an account, click "Add Business" and fill out the form with your business details. We'll review and approve your listing within 24-48 hours.

Is it free to list my business?

Yes! Basic business listings are completely free. We also offer premium features for businesses that want enhanced visibility.

How do reviews work?

Customers can leave reviews and ratings for businesses they've used. Reviews help other customers make informed decisions.

Can I edit my business listing?

Yes, you can edit your business information at any time by logging into your account and accessing your business dashboard.

How do I contact a business?

Each business listing includes contact information such as phone number, email, and website. You can contact them directly using these details.

What if I find incorrect information?

If you find incorrect information about a business, please contact us and we'll investigate and correct it as soon as possible.
